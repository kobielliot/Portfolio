Japanese Women's Leadership Initiative

Empowering Japanese women to become leaders and create social change in Japan.

Tell us your email, and we’ll notify you when applications open!

JWLI empowers Japanese women to become leaders

Develop leadership skills and empower current and future generations of women leaders in Japan.

Foster more active participation and engagement of women in Japanese civil society.

Based in Boston, JWLI was founded in 2006 and offers hands-on nonprofit management training with an entrepreneurial approach.
